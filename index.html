About Beatbench

Beatbench is a user-friendly digital audio workstation (DAW) designed to streamline music production, from uploading your tracks to applying AI-powered mastering. With features that allow genre selection, automatic mastering, and a variety of sampling packs, Beatbench simplifies the creation of high-quality music tracks.

Whether you’re a beginner or a seasoned producer, Beatbench provides the tools you need to craft your sound, combining simplicity with powerful functionality in a compact and efficient layout. The platform also includes an AI-driven automix feature, making it easier than ever to achieve professional-quality results in minutes.

Founded by Thabang Mosoana, previously known as Mahzbe and now going by the name TH Mpini, Beatbench reflects his passion for music and technology. TH Mpini’s vision was to create a DAW that caters to both novices and experts, offering an intuitive experience without compromising on the depth of control. His journey in the music industry has informed the design of Beatbench, aiming to empower creators at every level.
